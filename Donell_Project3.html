Instructions

     This is a game about a cat who takes care of you. When the game starts you'll be able to name your companion. Afterwards, each time you open the app your cat will provide you with uplifting and inspiring quotes as well as encourage you to do some basic self care related tasks. Your cat can keep up with your daily progress and record data over time about how your self care improves. For example, your cat will ask to to drink as much water as you to stay hydrated and you can tell her about how much water you've had at any time or even click on your cat to see her track your progress over time. 
   If you visit the in-app store you can purchase more cats to help specialize the program to meet your needs. You can buy recipe packs, exercise guides, relaxing music, and more!
